Doraemon

  • Doraemon is a robotic cat from the 22nd century.
  • He travels back in time to help a young boy named Nobita.
  • Doraemon has a four-dimensional pocket with futuristic gadgets.
  • He is one of the most beloved characters in Japanese manga and anime history.
  • The series teaches values like friendship, kindness, and creativity.
